Medibank
Health Solutions

Redesigning agent status management for a contact centre serving thousands of daily interactions — making availability visible, consistent, and effortless across every channel.

2023 · UX Lead · Contact Centre · Healthcare

The Situation

Agents shouldn't have to think about availability.

Medibank Health Solutions' contact centre agents handle voice calls, chat sessions, and emails simultaneously — all from a single browser-based workspace powered by Coral Active.

The problem was fragmentation. Being "ready" for a call didn't automatically mean being ready for chat. Status changes were manual and inconsistent per channel. Supervisors had no unified view of team availability — they couldn't tell at a glance who was truly available, who was at capacity, or why someone had gone offline.

In a contact centre, those blind spots compound. Misrouted interactions, delayed responses, and unclear queue states don't just frustrate agents — they directly affect customer satisfaction scores and SLA compliance.

The Approach

One decision. All channels.

The design centred on a single principle: agent availability should be set once and flow to every channel automatically. When an agent goes ready, they're ready for voice, chat, and email — unless they explicitly aren't, and they say why.

Status changes now come with reason codes — Break, Training, Coaching, Administration, and more — so supervisors always know the why, not just the what. Partial Ready states surface exactly which channel is unavailable, eliminating ambiguity when managing live queues.

The Results

Fewer decisions. Faster responses.

Agents no longer manage status per channel. One action updates all three — and routing handles the rest. Supervisors get a live view of the entire team without chasing anyone down.

Unified Status Widget

Single sidebar component — all channel states visible and editable without navigating away from the active workspace.

Reason Codes

10 structured reason codes for Not Ready states — Administration, Break, Coaching, Training, and more — giving supervisors actionable context.

Partial Ready Visibility

When one channel is unavailable, the widget surfaces exactly which one — and why — without requiring a supervisor to investigate.

One-Click Transfer

Agent View lets supervisors see each team member's channel availability and initiate a transfer in a single action from the same panel.

Conclusion

"Contact centre agents don't need more features — they need fewer decisions."

The Medibank Coral Active widget makes availability management invisible. Set it once, let routing handle the rest. Supervisors get live team context without chasing. Agents stay focused on the customer, not the tool.

In a high-volume contact centre, the difference between a good day and a bad one is often just how much mental overhead the system adds. This design removes it.
