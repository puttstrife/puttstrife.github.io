Star Seeker

Designing a recruitment website that helps companies hiring remotely communicate trust, process, and value clearly enough that the next candidate wants to keep going.

2023 · UI / UX Design · Recruitment · SaaS

Mobile View

Built for the first screen a user sees.

Star Seeker needs to earn trust quickly on mobile, so the opening screen starts with the pitch and the remaining screens carry the supporting story. Showing the content inside a device frame keeps the sequence feeling like a real phone experience instead of a stretched desktop page.

Trust at the top

The value proposition and call to action are visible immediately, so the opening screen does the heavy lifting.

Process in motion

Supporting screens keep the hiring story moving forward without making the user hunt for the next step.

Conversion stays clear

Resources and lead capture remain easy to scan, so the mobile path still feels calm and purposeful.

The Situation

Recruitment sites need to feel credible fast.

Star Seeker helps companies find remote workers, which means the site has to work hard in a very short amount of time. Visitors need to understand the service, trust the brand, and see a clear process without reading a long explanation.

The old challenge in hiring websites is familiar: too much generic language, too little structure, and not enough signal that the company understands how remote recruitment really works.

The Approach

A bright, simple story around the hiring journey.

The design leans into a bold purple identity with a clean editorial structure. The homepage leads with the value proposition, then expands into service cards, the recruitment process, testimonials, pricing, and lead capture so the story builds trust step by step.

Supporting pages keep the same tone on both desktop and mobile. The result is a hiring site that feels polished without becoming stiff, and friendly without losing credibility.

The Results

A recruitment story that reads with more confidence.

The redesign gives Star Seeker a clearer value proposition and a more credible visual system for remote hiring. The page flow helps visitors understand what the company does and why it is worth contacting.

Stronger first impression

The homepage makes the hiring promise obvious right away, without forcing visitors to hunt for the message.

Clear process story

Service, process, and testimonials create a narrative that explains how the recruitment engine works.

Mobile continuity

The same hierarchy and CTA flow carry through to mobile, where many recruitment visits begin.

More trust at the edge

Pricing, resources, and lead capture reinforce the brand as a serious recruitment partner.

Conclusion

"Good hiring sites don't just attract clicks - they reduce uncertainty."

Star Seeker is designed to feel clear, optimistic, and dependable. That combination helps the user move from curiosity to contact with less friction.
